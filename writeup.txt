# Mall of America Interactive Sales Experience — Write-up

## Overview

This project is a premium interactive sales experience for Mall of America, built for tenants, sponsors, restaurant groups, entertainment operators, and event partners.

The goal is to move beyond a normal website or linear presentation and create a digital sales tool that helps a prospect quickly understand the commercial value of being associated with Mall of America. The experience presents Mall of America as more than a shopping center — it positions it as a destination-scale platform for visibility, foot traffic, brand activations, events, dining, entertainment, and measurable business growth.

The experience is designed around one core idea:

> Mall of America is not just a place people visit. It is a place where brands can create attention, memory, and commercial opportunity at scale.

---

## Concept

The concept is built around a non-linear journey where every viewer can choose the path that matters most to them.

A retail tenant can explore flagship visibility and visitor demand.  
A luxury brand can explore premium positioning at scale.  
A restaurant group can understand how dining becomes part of a full-day destination experience.  
An entertainment operator can see how attractions create repeat traffic and emotional memory.  
An event partner can understand how a launch can become a public cultural moment.

Instead of forcing every prospect through the same fixed sequence, the experience lets them drive their own journey from a central hub.

---

## Experience Structure

The project starts with a cinematic hero section that introduces Mall of America as a major commercial destination. From there, the user enters a non-linear command hub.

The main sections are:

- **Retail** — focused on flagship visibility, foot traffic, and store discovery.
- **Luxury** — focused on premium positioning, exclusivity, and high-volume exposure.
- **Dining** — focused on restaurants becoming part of the destination experience.
- **Entertainment** — focused on attractions, repeat visits, and family traffic.
- **Events** — focused on launches, brand activations, and public moments.
- **AI Strategy Builder** — generates tailored partnership concepts.
- **ROI Moment** — converts visitor scale into a personalized business case.
- **Contact** — provides clear next steps for partnership conversations.

This makes the experience feel like an interactive sales platform rather than a slide deck.

---

## Visual and Motion Direction

The visual direction uses a premium black-and-gold system, large editorial typography, glass-style cards, cinematic gradients, subtle grain, animated transitions, and immersive video backgrounds.

The project is intentionally video-first. The hero and opportunity sections use motion backgrounds to create atmosphere and make each commercial path feel more emotional and memorable.

The background videos are AI-generated motion assets, and the AI atmosphere cards add additional visual concept frames such as:

- Luxury Arrival
- Sponsor Tunnel
- Event Stage

Together, these assets support the brief’s requirement for generative imagery, motion, and atmosphere.

---

## AI Integration

AI is used as a meaningful part of the sales experience, not just as a small add-on.

The **AI Strategy Builder** allows the viewer to choose:

- Partner type
- Opportunity zone
- Objective
- Atmosphere
- Immersion intensity

Based on those inputs, the app calls a backend Groq API route and generates a tailored activation strategy. The output includes a headline, concept, motion direction, visitor journey, and commercial logic.

This makes the experience feel personalized because the prospect receives an activation idea based on their own context instead of only reading static content.

A fallback strategy is included so the experience remains polished even if the API is temporarily unavailable during review or deployment.
